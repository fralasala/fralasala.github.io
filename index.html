How to have a good skincare routine

Some basic information

Why is skincare important? every day our skin is exposed to all type of weather such as sun, rain, wind but also pollution and these things can damage a lot and create acne and other problems.
How many steps do you have to do?We usually think that we need many products to have a good skincare routine, but that is completely wrong! Few steps are perfect and in this website we will teach you how to make them properly.
Expensive products are better? There is also the false myth that you must use very expensive products, even if there are a lot of cheap brands that are really good.

steps
